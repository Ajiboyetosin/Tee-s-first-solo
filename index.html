About this site

Hello


I'm Tosin and I'm a foodie.
I believe as we grow older, it is important we have the little things
that would always give us joy no matter what life throws at us.
I believe food is one common thing that can give the little joys of life no
matter how old we get. You remember how as a kid, a reward of something
edible always brought a bright smile to our faces, whether it was chocolate, ice cream, or chicken it always did the trick.
We immediately turned from whatever foul or naughty mood we
were to an happier one at the promise of food we liked.
I believe it would be rather sad to give a excuse of adulthood to lose the joy in food.
No matter what life throws at you, always eat first, cos I mean you need the energy to fight and face whatever it is.
Never be too old to light up at the sound of food,
never be too matured to salivate at the smell of good food.

Good food is the foundation of genuine happiness. Part of the secret of success is to eat what you like and let the food fight it out inside.
